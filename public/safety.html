Tips For Protesting

What To Wear:

Shatter proof goggles.

Broad face covering. Ex. Bandana

Proctive shoes, long shirt, and long pants.


What To Do:

Make a plan with your friends if you happen to get seperated

Watch for signs of physical and mental problems in yourself and others

Document: film or write down police actions, brutality, and injuries


What NOT to Do:

Don't wear anything oil based on your skin, it can trap tear gas and make the effects worse

Don't wear things that can be easily grabbed (i.e. jewelry, ties, loose hair)

Don't go alone, if you can help it.

Don't forget to eat food and dring lots of water
